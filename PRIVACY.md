# Privacy Policy

Quadstick: Config Manager does not collect, store, or share any personal
information. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

The app runs on your computer and works with the profile files you choose. The
only time it uses the network is when you paste a Google Sheets link to import a
profile. It then fetches the sheet you asked for and nothing else. It never
sends any information about you.

Questions? Open an issue at
https://github.com/Bbrizly/Quadstick-Config-Manager/issues.
