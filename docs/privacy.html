Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated 15 July 2026

Quadstick: Config Manager does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

What the app does with your data

The app runs entirely on your own computer and works with the profile files you choose to open. It does not create an account, it does not sign you in, and it does not send anything about you or your files anywhere.

The one time it uses the network

The only feature that touches the internet is the optional Google Sheets import. When you paste a Google Sheets link, the app fetches that sheet so it can bring the profile into the editor. It requests the sheet you asked for and nothing else. If you never use that feature, the app never uses the network at all.

Analytics and tracking

None. There is no telemetry, no usage reporting, no advertising, and no third-party SDK collecting data in the background.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
